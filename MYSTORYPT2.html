My third trimester appointments got started with my VBAC supportive doctor Dr.Sreedevi,Fernandez Hospital(Hyderabad,India).

My Doctor wasn't against VBAC but she always made me aware of the risks involved with it & said that it is you who can do it & not us.

I was more involved in everything of my pregnancy,I would make a to-do list of my diet & exercises everyday & keep myself motivated,my Amani childbirth educator & doula friend was Zahra Batool

And then came the preparation of my birthplan, My birthplan included things like, No episiotomy unless needed, skin to skin contact after birth, breastfeeding,

quiet environment, upright birthing position, freedom of movement to name a few.

I started discussing my birthplan with my doctor,she agreed to most of the things except to freedom of movement,as I was trying for TOLAC she wanted me to be monitored most of the time

I was also interested in meeting midwifery healthcare as I was more into having a pro natural birth.they were very supportive from the start & would always uplift me by appreciating my efforts. we talked with them & insisted on having intermittent monitoring during labor.

It was also generous of them to conduct a hypnobirthing session for me,although I am not much into hypnobirthing but a session of hypnobirthing during my labor really helped me progress my contractions & stay relaxed & focused.

My husband arrived just 10 days before my due date,my birth team was complete now.we would have sessions together with the doctor,midwives & my doula where in we discussed so much about labor.

As 9th month got started,I was taking about 7 dates a day,doing more of walking,squatting,stairs climbing, bouncing & swaying on ball,pelvic tilts & pelvic floor exercises(note-I've already been doing these exercises since my 30th week)

Also started with perineal massage at around 36 weeks twice a week,duck walk alternatively just 2 weeks before my due date along with accupressure massage techniques,essential oils (clary sage was my favourite),intercourse & nipple stimulation to induce labor.

I was having false pains since my 38th week but because they weren't progressing I was sure about them to be false.I was scheduled for a membrane sweep at about 39+5 days just 2 days before my due date.

Stay tuned to know about my labor story in the next part.
