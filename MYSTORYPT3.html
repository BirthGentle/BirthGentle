LABOR STORY

I was called for a membrane sweep at about 39+5 weeks & just before heading to the hospital my pains got started at about 5 to 10 minutes apart,10 am in the morning...I would hold on to one place & practice rhythmic breathing with every contraction.

They were slowing down a little in between.my doula was there with me in no time,she suggested me to have hot shower & squat in between the contractions when in shower.I followed her.I did my lunch,and was having dates & zamzam in between.

Contractions started coming every 5 minutes apart,I would bounce & walk easily at this moment.my doula helped me deal with my contractions using hot water bag,accupressure massager & put clary sage on my forehead that allowed me to calm down a little

We thought it's better to head to the hospital & get it checked at this moment.On examination they said that my cervix is only soft & not dilated & they sent us back home

We came back home at around & started making extra efforts for the labor to progress,.

Walking,stairs climbing,hips swaying,bouncing on ball,squatting & taking rest in between.my doula made sure of the environment I was desiring of,with some positive affirmations hanged on to the wall.I would read them & would say myself that I can do it back again.simultaneously I was also taking rest in between.

At around 11pm we noticed my contractions to be more stronger at about 1 to 2 minutes apart,we again drove back to the hospital..

I was checked & I was only 1 cm dilated,but they wanted me to be admitted as my contractions were very intense..

Intermittent monitoring got started.

I was already exhausted,I wasn't able to walk around much & do anything.I was depressed because I was only 1cm with this much of pain.)

An hour later I also had a session of hypnobirthing with the midwife of the hospital,I was calm,I was relaxed but with each contraction I would vocalize. They checked me back again & said that I was 2 cms now..We were happy that the contractions were progressing,we thanked the midwife for the session of hypnobirthing as it really helped me keep calm & release some more oxytocin.

I wasn't able to have anything at this moment,I was vomiting badly,I was put on IV. I was in pain the whole night with intermittent monitoring in between,I was mostly on bed,but tried hard moving around in between.

I would still vocalise with every contraction,at this moment the effect of hot water bag was nil as the pains were only getting intense & stronger.my doula would encourage me with positive words,my husband would play quran,I would take a nap listening to quran..

Early morning when they checked me back again at 5 am I was 4cms,sigh of relief.Waters broke at 9 am & soon I was 10 cms dilated in no time.

I started feeling the urge to push..my husband came forward to hold me from my back,felt like we worked as a team to have our baby out. midwives encouraged me to push at the right time & I could feel my baby breathing out.

I could feel my baby’s head soon,I started gaining my energy after feeling my baby.I gave all of my determination to have the baby out.

Everyone shouted yes,yes you can do it,baby is almost out & it was just one last push. It was around 12:29pm baby entered into this world,they laid her on my chest,she could feel the warmth of her mumma & started crying.

Happiness rolled around the room,it was the most beautiful moment of my life to hold my baby in my arms & with husband by my side.

Midwives checked for any tears during birth as episiotomy wasn’t done,they weren't any Alhamdulillah.It was a complete natural birth with no medications & intact perineum.All praise is to Allah alone.

we were also keen about delayed cord clamping, Daddy was happy to cut the cord.

We made it till here,I believed in the power of dua,I thanked Allah,I thanked everyone from my birth team,without them this wouldn't have been possible.
